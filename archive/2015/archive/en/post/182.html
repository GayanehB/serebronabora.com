Font Licensing

Font design will be examined as an object of intellectual property.  Font piracy, protection, as well as legal rights of type designers, type foundries, and end users will be discussed. We shall cover the meaning behind font licenses, as well as their types. We shall also discuss some prominent  lawsuits dealing with font design in the US, Europe, and Russia.
