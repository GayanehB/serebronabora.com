sponsorship

As a new venture conceived by a team of enthusiasts, the conference welcomes your support. Your contributions will make our ideas possible and allow us to bring in top speakers. Your contributions are essential to making our plans happen and will go a long way to raise visibility for your business. We expect attendees from all over Russia, as well as form a number of neighboring countries.

Our conference has been supported by the leading design professionals. We have lined up presentations by the top names in Russian typography and type design. The conference stands to become a significant event in Russian design life.

Conference information, including sponsor list, will be available at http://serebronabora.com, as well as on online design publications and popular social networks. It will be covered by major design periodicals. The event is an ideal place to advertise to designers, since the target audience is planning to attend it en masse. Available sponsorship packages are listed below. We will be also happy to consider other offers.
